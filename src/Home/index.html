About Us

Founded in 1985, First Alarm LLC has evolved from a humble beginning into a leading Security Solutions Provider, offering comprehensive services across Massachusetts and Southern New Hampshire. Our expertise spans both residential and commercial sectors, including home automation systems, and we have established strong partnerships with New Home Builders and Remodelers.

Boasting nearly four decades of experience, First Alarm LLC is celebrated for its unwavering commitment to community safety and customer satisfaction. Our reputation for excellence is not just reflected in the trust and loyalty of our clients, but also in the professional accolades we've received for our quality services. At First Alarm LLC, protecting your peace of mind is at the heart of everything we do.
